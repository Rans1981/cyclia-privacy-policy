Privacy at Cyclia

Last updated: April 20, 2026

✅ Privacy Summary 2.0

📱 Local Data Your health data is saved ONLY on your phone. It is NEVER sold or sent to servers for external processing.
🛡️ Anonymous Analytics We use Firebase to improve the app, but it is 100% anonymous and does NOT include period dates or symptoms.
🚫 Zero Sale Your data is sacred. We will never share it with insurers, employers, or marketing agencies.
🔐 Total Control You can export your data to PDF or delete your account and all analytics traces with a single tap in Settings.
⚠️ CLINICAL NOTE: Cyclia is an AI-based wellness tool, not a certified medical device. Always consult a professional before making health decisions.

1. Information We Collect

Cyclia minimizes data collection as much as possible. Data is divided into two categories:

1.1 Sensitive Data (Local)

This data is under your sole control and does not leave your device:

  • Menstrual calendar and log dates.
  • Symptoms (pain, mood, energy, etc.).
  • Basal Body Temperature (BBT).
  • Personal notes and medical reminders.

1.2 Technical Data (Anonymous)

Information collected to ensure the functioning of the app:

  • Device model and OS version.
  • Error reports (Crashlytics).
  • Interaction patterns (which screens you use most).

2. Data Minimization in Analytics

Complying with Google Play (April 2026) guidelines for High Sensitivity data, we have implemented strict measures:

  • No Exact Dates: We do not send period start dates or future prediction dates to our analytics.
  • No Symptoms: We do not track which specific symptoms you record.
  • Instance Anonymization: Upon deleting the account, a total reset of Firebase Analytics IDs is triggered to guarantee your future anonymity.

3. ARCO Rights and Deletion

You have absolute control over your personal data:

  • Access: All information is visible to you in the Dashboards.
  • Portability: You can generate detailed PDF reports at any time.
  • Deletion: The "Delete account and data" option deletes the local database and revokes analytics identifiers.

4. Security Standards

We implement AES-256 encryption in exports and protect local access through native biometric systems (Fingerprint/Face) on your device if you so configure it.
